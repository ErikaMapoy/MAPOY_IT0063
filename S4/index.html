WHOLE FRIED CHICKEN

Category: Max's Filipino Fried Chicken

Location: Ortigas Center, Pasig, Metro Manila, Philippines

Open: 10AM - 9PM

The iconic Max’s Fried Chicken that made it a Filipino institution! Marinated in secret spices, deep-fried to a golden crisp, and served with banana ketchup and Worcestershire sauce, every bite is Sarap-to-the-Bones. The tender meat and crispy skin make it perfect for family meals or gatherings!

Ingredients: Whole chicken, secret spices, salt, pepper, oil, garlic, onion powder, Worcestershire sauce, banana ketchup.

Price: ₱545.00

CRISPY PATA (CRISPY PORK HOCK)

Category: Ruby's Favorites

Location: Ortigas Center, Pasig, Metro Manila, Philippines

Open: 10AM - 9PM

A Filipino favorite, this premium pork knuckle is slow-cooked, marinated, and deep-fried to golden perfection. The crispy skin and juicy meat make it irresistible! Served with a savory soy-vinegar sauce and atchara, this hearty dish is best enjoyed with rice and shared among family and friends.

Ingredients: Pork knuckle, garlic, bay leaves, peppercorns, salt, oil, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, atchara, onion.

Price: ₱899.00

KARE-KARE (BEEF PEANUT STEW)

Category: Ruby's Favorites

Location: Ortigas Center, Pasig, Metro Manila, Philippines

Open: 10AM - 9PM

A rich, creamy peanut stew made with tender beef chunks, fresh vegetables, and annatto oil. Drizzled with ground peanuts and spring onions, this dish is best paired with Max’s signature sautéed bagoong for a savory kick. Good for 3-4 persons, this classic Filipino favorite is a must-try!

Ingredients: Beef chunks, peanut sauce, annatto oil, eggplant, string beans, banana blossoms, garlic, onions, bagoong, salt.

Price: ₱799.00

SIZZLING TOFU

Category: Ruby's Favorites

Location: Ortigas Center, Pasig, Metro Manila, Philippines

Open: 10AM - 9PM

A house favorite, this dish features crispy tofu cubes tossed in a creamy, umami-rich sauce and topped with chilies for a slight spicy kick. Served sizzling hot, it’s perfect for sharing as an appetizer or main dish. A great vegetarian-friendly option with bold, satisfying flavors!

Price: ₱375.00

SINIGANG NA HIPON

Category: Soups

Location: Ortigas Center, Pasig, Metro Manila, Philippines

Open: 10AM - 9PM

A comforting, tangy soup featuring plump shrimp, fresh vegetables, and a tamarind-based broth. This savory-sour dish includes radish, string beans, and kangkong, creating a perfect balance of flavors. Served piping hot, it's a refreshing, heartwarming favorite best enjoyed with steamed rice.

Ingredients: Shrimp, tamarind broth, string beans, radish, eggplant, tomatoes, onions, kangkong, fish sauce, green chili.

Price: ₱565.00

PANCIT BIHON (RICE NOODLES)

Category: Soups

Location: Ortigas Center, Pasig, Metro Manila, Philippines

Open: 10AM - 9PM

A classic Filipino noodle dish made with stir-fried rice noodles, tender meat, shrimp, and vegetables. Infused with garlic, soy sauce, and seasonings, this savory, flavorful favorite is perfect for celebrations or everyday meals. Served with calamansi for a zesty kick, this dish is comfort food at its best!

Ingredients: Bihon noodles, chicken, pork, shrimp, cabbage, carrots, green beans, garlic, onions, soy sauce, calamansi.

Price: ₱455.00
